  • 1

    Nothing to install

    Opens in the browser the technician already uses — no app store, no hardware.

  • 2

    One-tap start / stop

    Tap to begin a job; the phone's GPS reports automatically every 10–15 seconds.

  • 3

    Works through dead zones

    If signal drops, readings queue on the phone and send the moment the connection returns.

  • 4

    Live distance

    Miles driven for the job tick up automatically, measured from the real track.

  • 5

    Screen-awake

    The page keeps the screen on during a job to avoid losing GPS while mounted.

How the gap is filled

During the 5-minute blackout the phone records nothing, so the route is estimated from the last-known point toward the known job destination at road-route pace (shown dashed). The instant the phone is back online, that segment is re-estimated between the two real points and its distance is flagged estimated — never mixed with measured miles.

Mockup only · colours and figures are illustrative. Estimated distance is kept separate so it can be excluded from any billing.
